David Horovich was born in London to Alice Mary, a teacher, and Morris Horovich, a childcare worker. He was educated at St Christopher's School, an independent boarding school in Letchworth Garden City in Hertfordshire, and later at Central School speeches and drama in London.

Horovich's father was Jewish but an atheist and married a non-Jewish woman and Horovich was not raised in the faith.

He is best known for his role as Inspector Slack in Miss Marple. He is currently starring in the Game of Thrones prequel The House of the Dragon as Grand Maester Mellos.
