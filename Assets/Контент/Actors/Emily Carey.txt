Emily Carey was born on April 30, 2003 in North London. She made her professional stage debut at the age of 9, playing the roles of young Shrek and young Princess Fiona in the musical Shrek, staged at London's Drury Lane Theatre. She then played Martha von Trapp in The Sound of Music at Regent's Park Open Air Theatre. The production was nominated for a Laurence Olivier Award (2014) for Best Musical Revival. Emily repeated her performance at the awards ceremony held at the Royal Opera House, Covent Garden.
