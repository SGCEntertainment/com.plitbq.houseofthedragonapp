Ewan Robert Mitchell (born 8 March 1997) is an English actor. He is known for his roles in the ITV historical drama The Quiet (2017), the medieval drama The Last Kingdom (2017–2022), the BBC World War II series World on Fire (2019–), and the HBO fantasy series "Dragon House (2022–). He also appeared in the Trigger Point series (2022) and the Claire Denis film High Society (2018).
