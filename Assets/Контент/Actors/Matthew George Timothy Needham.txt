Matthew George Timothy Needham - born April 13, 1984 British actor. He made his television debut as Toby de Silva in the BBC soap opera Casualty (2007-2009).

Needham has performed on stage with the Royal Shakespeare Company, the Royal Court Theatre, the Royal National Theatre, the Almeida Theater and Shakespeare's Globe Theatre. In 2011, he was nominated for the Ian Charleson Award.

In 2015, Needham starred in the short film Stutterer, written and directed by Benjamin Cleary and produced by Serena Armitage and Shan Christopher Ogilvy. The film won the Best Short Film award at the 88th Academy Awards.

In January 2017, Needham appeared in Episode 2 Episode 4 of the ITV drama series Endeavor "Song of Songs" as Dudley Kavanagh. In March, he played Stanley Kowalski in Tennessee Williams' A Streetcar Named Desire on BBC Radio 3.

Needham played Mr Crow in the first episode of the ITV historical drama Sanditon. Since 2022, he has been playing Larissa Strong in the HBO fantasy series House of the Dragon.
