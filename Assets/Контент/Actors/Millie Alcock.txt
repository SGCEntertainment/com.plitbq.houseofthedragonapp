Millie Alcock was born in 2001 in Australia. At the age of 13, she first appeared on the screen, playing a cameo role in the Australian TV series Wonderland. Her professional television career began in 2017, when Millie began to appear regularly in various series and small films.

So far, all the roles she has played are limited to local Australian projects, the most famous and recognized of which was the comedy series The Piano. It was presented at the Australian Academy of Film and Television Awards and won in the category "Best Comedy Series". Millie Alcock's co-star Tim Minchin was honored as "Best Comedy Performer".
