Rhys Ivans was born on July 22, 1967 in the small town of Haverfordwest, located in southwest Wales. His mother, Beti-Vin, was a kindergarten teacher, and his father, Eurvin, was an elementary school teacher. The actor's childhood passed in the town of Ritin, where he graduated from elementary school. Ivans received his secondary education in the nearby city of Mold, and there he began attending acting classes, which took place at the local theater. After leaving school, he got a job at S4C, where he hosted various programs in the Welsh language. Ivans later entered the Guildhall School of Music and Drama (London) in the department of acting and graduated in 1997.
