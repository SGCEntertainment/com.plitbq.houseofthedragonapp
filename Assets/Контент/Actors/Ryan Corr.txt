Ryan Corr was born in Melbourne. His father is Peter Corr, head coach of the Australian women's goalball team. Ryan began his acting career at the age of thirteen in the short film "Opraholic", the first experience in the series was the role of Matthew McDougal in the series "The Night Party". After that, the actor got the lead role of Shan Zamette in the TV series Silver Sun. In 2006, he starred in the TV series The Big Wave.

In 2009 he graduated from the National Institute of Dramatic Arts. In 2018, he starred in the film Mary Magdalene as Joseph.

In 2022, he made a cameo appearance as Ser Harvin Strong in the TV series House of the Dragon.
