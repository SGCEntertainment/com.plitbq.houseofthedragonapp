Tom Glynn-Carney (born 7 February 1995) is an English actor and singer. He appeared in Christopher Nolan's war film Dunkirk (2017), Tolkien, the King and Rialto (2019) and as King Aegon II Targaryen in The House of the Dragon (2022).

Glynn-Carney is also the lead singer of the indie band Sleep Walking Animals.
